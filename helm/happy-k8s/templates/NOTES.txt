To check the status of your agents:

  kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "happy-k8s.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

To view logs from an agent:

  kubectl logs -f {{ include "happy-k8s.fullname" . }}-0 -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

To access an agent shell:

  kubectl exec -it {{ include "happy-k8s.fullname" . }}-0 -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -- /bin/bash

To trigger a rolling restart (for updates):

  kubectl rollout restart statefulset/{{ include "happy-k8s.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

Connect to your agents via the Happy mobile app using your configured credentials.
